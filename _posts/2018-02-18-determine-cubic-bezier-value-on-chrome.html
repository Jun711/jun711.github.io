Chrome - cubic-bezier chart


Steps

1) Find a page or your own page that has animation in the CSS.
2) Turn on Chrome developer tools and go to styles tab.
3) Click on the easing logo in the animation line.
4) Cubic-bezier draggable chart will open up.
