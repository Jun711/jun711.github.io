  • It is really user friendly. It displays nearby public transportation when you pan the map around.
  • It contains accurate live transit information of transportation schedule.
  • It contains data for most forms of transportation in Vancouver: skytrains, buses, ferries, car2go etc.
  • You can save locations for future reference and navigation.
